FAQs & Help



Frequently Asked Questions

  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.
  • How can I track my order?
    You will receive a tracking number once your order has placed.
  • What is your return policy?
    We offer a 30-day return policy for all products.
  • What is your privacy policy?
    We take your privacy seriously and will never share your personal information with third parties. Please see our Privacy Policy page for more information.
  • What is your refund policy?
    We offer refunds for products that are returned within 30 days of purchase. Please see our Refund Policy page for more information.
  • Can I change or modify my order?
    If you need to change or modify your order, please contact us as soon as possible with your order number. We will do our best to accommodate your request.
  • How can I contact customer support?
    You can email us at support@flickker.com or use the 'Contact Us' form on our website. Our customer support team is available 24/7.

Help

  • How do I contact customer support?
    You can email us at support@flickker.com
  • How do I place an order?
    Select the product you wish to purchase, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will be prompted to enter your shipping and payment information before completing your order.
  • How long does shipping take?
    Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose. Standard shipping usually takes 3-7 business days, while expedited shipping can arrive in 1-3 business days.
  • Do you offer international shipping?
    Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary.
  • What should I do if I receive a damaged or defective item?
    Please contact our customer support team with your order number and a description of the issue. We will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
  • How do I cancel an order?
    If you need to cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible with your order number. We will do our best to cancel the order before it ships.
